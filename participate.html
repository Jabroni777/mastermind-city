Questions We Ask Early

How is this different from a normal online community?

Output is mandatory. Pods are structured around deliverables, implementation timelines, and accountability loops.

How are contributions rewarded?

Through explicit mechanisms: equity participation, financing access, governance influence, DAO treasury payouts, and preferred internal market opportunities.

Is this anti-society or separatist?

No. The goal is lawful, functional institution building that people opt into because it works better, not because they are forced.

Where do autonomous agents fit in?

Agents reduce complexity by helping members navigate rules, opportunities, and decision pathways without replacing human accountability.
